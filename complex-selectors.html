Complex selectors

All complex selectors follow the next syntax:

Syntax:
Selector1 combinator1 Selector2 combinator2 ... combinatorN-1 SelectorN

Where each selector can be any compound selector and the SelectorN, known as the key selector, identifies the elements that the rule applies to.

A compound selector selects the elements that simultaneously matches all the given selectors.
